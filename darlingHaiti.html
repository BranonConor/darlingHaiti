Why Us?

We're passionate.

Each and every member of our team commits their time to volunteering out of sheer passion. As folks with day jobs, families, and many other commitments, we choose year after year to lend a helping hand because we love Haiti and its beautiful people.

We're experienced.

Dr. Anderson, as well as many team members, have returned to volunteer in Haiti over 20 times. As experienced healthcare professionals and as frequent visitors to Haiti, our experience with addressing the needs in this region is strong.

We're connected.

Through the many trips already under our belt, we've developed close relationships with many locals who work hard to catalyze our efforts. These trips would not be possible without the counsel and aid provided by our local Haitian partners.
